Written by Igbo Focus 17th November 2024 Where there are many sinners, God will always choose the least sinful ones. Even though according to them, Donald Trump is the most sinful man in America at the moment but God has preferred him to lead the country than those sneaky Democrats who claimed to be above suspicion of everything. During the election, some sections of the United States (US) were crying out there is Russian or China involvement in their election yet they didn’t stop to think about Nigeria and other countries’ elections that US were deeply involved in. They will tell you Nigeria didn’t matter because the country is not a super power or is a third world country so it doesn’t matter. Or Nigeria is nothing to go by, we can use them and nobody will complain. For that reason, we (the west) or super power must make sure no country in Africa becomes super power so that we can use them anyway we want. Just recently, I saw President Joe Biden making a mockery of Nigerians by saying their president has been dead for a long time and they don’t know about it. Yet the man or president he is talking about is the man they pushed on Nigerians as their president. Just look at the recent Nigeria election, US was involved by circuitously navigating the outcome using FBI and others. For a start, if US mean well for Nigeria, the US would have disclosed criminal offences committed in the US by the person seeking to become Nigeria president rather than leaving Nigerians scouting for information about him. And using him as a bargaining tool. The Chicago State University was uncooperative about the degree he said to have studied. US Drug Enforcement Administration was uncooperative, FBI was uncooperative, etc. So, because of the US and perhaps what they might have done left a lot of Nigerians baffled. Even, some people kept on saying that the US was behind him (the man) running for president. In the year 2015, the US was behind the election of Muhammadu Buhari, we all know what the US done then. This led to then the former president saying that “Barrack Obama interfered with the 2015 General Election in Nigeria.” He did that unambiguously. He constructed the entire Buhari firestorm that Nigerians endured. Beyond Libya Invasion & destabilising the Sahel, Obama & John Kerry had no business meddling in Nigeria. At the time, John Kerry was travelling to Nigeria up and down like US colonised Nigeria. An insight to what Barrack Obama did during the 2015 general election to mess up Nigeria. The explanation is by Professor Jona Onuoha who explained what happened through Felix Ikem’s write- up: Professor Jona Onuoha has given an insight on what he believed was the reason the then American President, Barack Obama meddled in the 2015 general election against former President Goodluck Jonathan. Prof. Onuoha who is the Director, Center for American Study, University of Nigeria and Professor of Political Science, said the former president of Nigeria was economical with the truth and wondered why he was not able to explain to the world through his book, “My Transition Hours” why Obama did what he did. “Incidentally, Jonathan was not able to explain why Obama did what he did, I can tell you what happened. “American has been showing much interest in domestic affairs of most countries in Africa, while the British Government came with the Bible in one hand and gun in the other hand, American government replaced the Bible with carrots, the carrots is the foreign aid, but God is with them. So, most governments that have not been able to implement American’s foreign policy faced sanctions and regime change; Jonathan’s case was one of them. “There were two major things that happened during Obama’s administration. I will explain one, which is the most important. Obama moved around and lobbied United Nations members to solicit their support to get a two-state structure for Israeli – Palestinian. Jonathan assured him of Nigeria’s support. What Obama needed was nine country’s votes out of the fifteen to have a two- state structure. But another group came to Jonathan and convinced him that voting for a two- state structure would be a fight against Israel and Christians. “At the end, Nigeria voted against the two-state structure which left Obama with eight votes against his targeted nine vote. “So, Obama saw that as a big betrayal despite America’s huge contributions to Nigeria and Africa’s development, that was when he plotted the regime change that would usher in a Nigerian president that would support and respect American foreign policy. He went further to place arms sale embargo on Jonathan’s government, making it difficult for him to fight Boko Haram. “Jonathan saw the handwriting on the wall that the 2015 election war against him was not only within the country, that was even the major reason he conceded defeat before the final result was announced. “Although, Jonathan is not the only victim of Obama’s plotted regime change, if you remember what happened in Libya during the time of Muammar Gaddafi you will find Obama’s foreign policy in Africa very brutal in economic terms.” As you can see then, President Goodluck Jonathan was up against odds he couldn’t possibly scale through, even if he made an effort. At the time of Goodluck Jonathan, Nigeria was said to be one of the fastest growing economies before Barack Obama brought in Muhammadu Buhari of the APC, this is a man that Nigerians refused to vote for, five times but he still came and mess things up for Nigerians. It is always the case with the Democrats who are very cunning, nothing to offer Africa, their only plan for Africa is malicious. Democrats continuously make certain of the encouragement immoral things in Africa such as religious wars, ethnic, tribalism, dictatorial, corruption, weak and very old leaders in African to manipulate. According to Nefertiti “Obama's Foreign Policy was a scourge to the entire African continent. But many don't see it (because he cocooned himself with a conceited public facade and spectacle). He created this whole cool guy persona for himself. He can fool many, but not Nefertiti. He aided Nigerians in their 2015 Wankfest. Millions wanted CHANGE, & they got it. Nigerians thought they found elixir; the fifth element, the quintessence of life. People thought they found the solution to Nigeria's woes. Chibok and Bring Back Our Girls (BBOG) was the result. He installed Buhari using the instruments of Psych-Op or elaborate brainwashing. The 'victims' of that Operation are ignorant till this day. They thought it was their individual brilliance and loud voices that brought about the CHANGE; but something more clandestine was happening behind the scenes. Someone was pressing the right button, directing the whole 'Zombie Fest.' CHANGE came quite alright, but in the opposite direction. Many question their tomfoolery till this day. Professors, the civil societies, the intelligentsia, and those that should have been wiser, fell for a well-oiled local propaganda that took on an international dimension. According to the English Lexicon, 'red meat' is something substantial, that can satisfy a basic need or appetite. Nigerians needed red meat, something to excite them; and they got it. “Sai Baba!" Love Affair was born. Quite early on, he destroyed Libya, and created the conflict in the Sahel. Wherever he went, he left a wave of destruction and carnage on a massive scale. He installed a man without a degree and pedigree. A man that wallowed in rampant obscurity since his ouster in 1985. Then he did the strangest thing by ignoring Nigeria for 8 wholesome years. Obama never stepped foot in Nigeria. That is who he is. A snake in green grass, hiding in plain sight. Back in Nigeria………. Jonathan was accused of been the brain behind the rise of the Boko Haram. He was accused of reducing the population of the North, so he could win that election. Back then, Isa Pantami was the “crying Sheikh.” Jonathan saw hell. Obama refused to sell arms to Nigeria. Goodluck Ebele Jonathan (GEJ) did the next best thing. He sorted for arms from the black market, but it was too late. By the time the army started the onslaught against the ragtag army, Jonathan’s fate was already sealed. And when the frenzy died down, Nigerians moved on, without knowing what went down behind the scenes. GEJ then made that famous speech that no political watcher will forget. “My ambition is not worth the blood of any Nigerian.” He conceded to preserve the peace and the precious unity the then Opposition wanted to destroy. I won’t forget “the blood of baboons” in a hurry. (Bring Back Our Girls Crusade) will remain my point of disagreement with Hadiza Bala Usman, Aisha Yesufu and Oby Ezekwesili. I and Aunty Oby blocked each till this day. Pulse NG & I are blocking each other as well. Pulse and many blogs ran a bunch of PR for the APC back then. I may appear naive sometimes, but I’m no fool. I choose my battles, and I chose them wisely.” Thank God that Donald Trump Has Been Reprieved otherwise who knows what another Democrats’ president would plot for Africa. Prior to the US election during 2016, everybody’s Chi reckons that Donald Trump will be the president of the US that will support Biafra restoration. And during the US November 2016 election, Biafrans were more hopeful for Donald Trump to win the election and help them restore Biafra Republic after being elected as a president. Throughout the election night of November 2016, many Biafrans streamed down to their churches, meditation centres, basilicas, bible reading bases, etc., on vigil to pray for Donald Trump in winning 2016 presidential election. Many of them even fasted for sometimes before the election and prayed heavily for Donald Trump. At the end of the 2016 election, luckily God answered everyone’s prayers, Donald Trump eventually won the election and was inaugurated on the 20th of January 2017 as the 45th President of the United States of America. Why did Biafrans became interested in Donald Trump being elected as a president of the United States of America (USA)? For me, I wasn’t interested in American election, I don’t even know Donald Trump or well enough to know what he is capable of but one day, a familiar voice said to me “there would be a man going to be US President, he will help Biafra gain independence.” I decided to desk research Donald Trump but the more I researched Donald Trump, the more the sign is showing that Donald Trump would not be the president but instead Hillary Clinton would be the next president. The only thing that I could find that connects Biafra with Donald Trump is that Donald Trump’s uncle was one of those that protested over the killings going on during the Biafra war. Right away, I determined to research Hillary Clinton but the more I delve into Hillary Clinton, the more disturbing facts I was discovering about her, duty and the Democrats in Africa including crisis like Boko Haram. The more I was trying to assimilate and scrutinise all the facts, I could notice that more and more Biafrans are reinvigorating for Donald Trump. Magnificent, it seemed that most Biafrans heard the same voice I heard that Donald Trump will help Biafra gain independence. Moreover, Mazi Nnamdi Kanu (MNK) consent to Donald Trump, thus MNK must have heard the same voice that we all heard, Donald Trump will help Biafra gain independence. At this point, Biafrans got the message and started taking sides, exhibiting support for Donald Trump. Right through the election night of November 2016, many Biafrans pour out to their churches, meditation centres, basilicas, bible reading bases, etc., on vigil to pray for Donald Trump in winning 2016 presidential election. Many of them even fasted for weeks before the election, prayed deeplyfor Donald Trump. As for me, I didn’t believe that Donald Trump would win as Hillary Clinton was the one leading in the polls. Not until the same voice said to me “cheer up” Donald Trump has won; he will be announced winner of US Presidential election tomorrow. In my mind, I wondered why Donald Trump would win when Hillary Clinton was the one leading in the polls. The same voice replied if Donald Trump was the one leading in the polls, he would have been frustrated out – I made it this way. The following day, Donald Trump was announced the winner of US Presidential election. It was a happy occasion for Trump supporters and Biafrans, though Nigeria government was worried. Since Nigeria civil war, Nigeria government have been instructed how to keep US and other governments sweet. Accordingly, the US wanted to get involve during Biafra war, but they were awarded contracts to keep them sweet. Biafrans gave up during the Nigeria Biafra war when Biafrans realised that Nigeria government was corrupting so many countries to get involve to fight Biafra by bribing them in terms of awarding contracts for them to be relevant or not to get involved. Egyptians were awarded contract to be bombing Biafra. Cameroon was offered part of Cross River State - Bakassi Peninsula in order for them to block their border from Biafra. Chad and Niger soldiers were drafted in on contract to fight Biafra from the other side. Recently I heard about the contract awarded to Israel to get them involve during Biafra war. There were so many countries Nigeria government bribed to get them to participate in Nigeria Biafra war and they had the effrontery of calling it “civil war.” Being that Nigeria government was bothered about the US election and the selection of Donald Trump that he might necessitate in giving support for Biafra independence. Nigeria government decide to place order for Tucano fighter jets and weapons just to please Donald Trump. Put it this way, President Muhammadu Buhari is a good friend of Barrack Obama and even Barrack Obama helped to install him in as the president. So, if President Muhammadu Buhari genuinely wanted to place order for Tucano fighter jets and weapons, he would have done it during President Barrack Obama’s administration just to please him. At the time of placing the order with Donald Trump many people warned Trump not sell the jets and weapons to President Muhammadu Buhari, cautioning Trump that Buhari will use them to kill innocent people but Donald Trump didn’t listen. As Buhari told him, he will use them to fight Boko Haram. Today those jets and weapons are not used to bomb or fight Boko Haram but used to bomb, fight and kill innocent people. Thank God that Donald Trump Has Been Reprieved otherwise who knows what another Democrats’ president would plot against Africa. Prior to the end of the first term of Donald Trump’s administration, he was prompted about helping Biafra through self- determination; accordingly, “I heard he replied that a lot of his aids and ministers are into oil business, they want to make money first before talking about Biafra, I talk about Biafra during my second tenure.” During Donald Trump’s second term election, Biafrans no longer interested wether he wins or not. Like before Biafrans didn’t streamed down to their churches, meditation centres, basilicas, bible reading bases, etc., on vigil to pray or fasted for Donald Trump. Failure to secure second term and all the recommencements afterwards seemed like God wants to tell Donald Trump something. Thenceforth wants to steer him to the right direction. Winning the last election means that there are some tasks God wants Donald Trump to accomplish. Thus, it seemed God has reprieved Donald Trump.

Thank God, Donald Trump Has Reprieved
