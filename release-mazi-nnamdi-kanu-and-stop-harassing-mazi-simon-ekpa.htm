IGBOFOCUS

Written by Oguchi Martins Egbujor 26 th January 2025 Everyone wants to cash in on the Igbos and their oil: Nigeria, Britain, France and others too numerous to mention. Kanu and Ekpa have scored many points against Nigeria and Britain. They are the winners, not losers. Nigeria is using Igbo wealth to build the north and the west to the detriment of the Igbo. Wealthy political thieves are clapping and supporting, including the clueless deputy at the house of representatives who praised Tinubu for doing nothing for the Igbos. Britain can lead the world to gang up against the Igbos as much as they want however, Biafra has come to stay from the first day APC stole the power from PDP. Anybody can cash in on oil well but Igbos have learnt not to depend on oil for survival. Nigeria used Igbo money to build rail line up to Buhari's village in Niger Republic but none for the Igbos. Tinubu started building gas pipe lines from Igbo but bypass the entire Igboland. To you, all Nigerians and your government, the spirit of more than 95% of Igbos have left Nigeria; you can enjoy your Nigeria as much as you want, we are no longer interested! Those of your Igbo politicians in Abuja cannot even force anyone in their village to do anything as Igbo culture sets everyone free in Igboland. We are not dominated by anyone, rich man or politicians. In Igboland, everyone is equal hence our problem with Nigerians who love to be dominated. Igbos yearn for freedom in Nigeria; we can feel it however, detaining Nnamdi Kanu and Simon Ekpa adds more impetus to the demand for Biafra. Our governors are governing themselves, their families and their cronies; even some of the governors live in Abuja. The Igbos would not even vote for Peter Obi in 2027 because he is a Nigerian, and supports the government that kills our youths in Orlu and Okigwe and other areas. Nigeria should release Mazi Nnamdi Kanu; that is the only thing that the Igbos care about in Nigeria presently. My name remains Oguchi Martins Egbujor, your messenger of truth. I have said it as the spirit of the Igbos have told me to. Please release Mazi Nnamdi Kanu to us. He who has ear let him hear!
Release Mazi Nnamdi Kanu And Stop Harassing Mazi Simon Ekpa
