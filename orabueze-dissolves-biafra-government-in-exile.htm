Orabueze Dissolves Biafra Government In Exile
Written by Igbo Focus 16th February 2025 Dr. Ngozi Orabueze, the Deputy Prime Minister and Chief of Staff of the United States of Biafra (USB) has dissolved Biafra Government in Exile. Despite the fact that it has been anticipated that after redeclaration of Biafra in Finland, the Biafra Government in Exile will be dissolved. The dissolution of Biafra Government in Exile was meant to be handled by Mazi Simon Ekpa the Prime Minister but now his case has been extended or behind schedule which has been projected to start from May 2025 when Nigeria Government will present their case to Finnish authority against the Prime Minister, Simon Ekpa. One thing Finnish authority doesn’t understand is that Nigeria Government’s intention is to just waste their time to delay things like the case of Mazi Nnamdi Kanu. Nigeria government intends to hold people challenging them in custody until Nigeria becomes Islamic state. They are doing that with Mazi Nnamdi Kanu, now they intend to do it with Simon Ekpa. They would have done it with Peter Obi if after winning the election and he pulled a fuss to rule the country, they would have put in prison until they attain Islamic state. In the book of Dr Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala, the Director General of World Trade Organisation, she said “whilst she was the Managing Director at the World Bank and had a request to come back to Nigeria to help fight corruption. She agreed because the nation is her country. As she came back and became the finance minister, the corrupt ones asked her to resign assignment and when she didn’t, they kidnapped her mother, held her for five days.” So, all you have to be to be arrested as a Nigerian is to be an innocent person to be jailed.
