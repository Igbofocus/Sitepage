About Us
Welcome to Igbo Focus We are delighted that you are interested to know much about us otherwise you wouldn't have been reading this page right now. We hope you will accept our greetings and appreciativeness for talking or writing about ourselves. Igbo Focus is all about Igbo people and the community. Igbo people will reap absolutely the full benefit of the Igbo Focus while those that are none Igbo people will acquire valuable information and better understanding of Igbo people and the community. We set sight on to structuring Igbo Focus website in a way that once you are at the site you will feel at home - that is if you’re an Igbo person but if you’re not Igbo person, you will still be able to enjoy the site after all it is written in English that most people understand. In view of that, the site is for those wishing to access Igbo news, information, entertainment and much more. Igbo Focus which commenced in the beginning of the millennia or to put in another way, Igbo Focus which started precisely in year 2000 to facilitate Igbo people’s correlated longings in inspiration, advancement, news, information and awareness. Since commencement, Igbo Focus has been publishing community news, children books, creating videos, online news, information pamphlets, messages and guidance. Igbo focus in anticipation, desired to continue all the above and much more especially with the hope of escorting those young Igbos born in Europe wishing to visit Igbo land. Many youths of Igbo descendant born in Europe have never visited Igbo land. Although many of the youths would like to visit Igbo land or their fatherland but are unable to do so due to security risks. Parents are fearful of security risks, bad roads, shortage of water, no lights, etc., decide to postpone their travel to Igbo land with their kids hoping that things will get better for good and before they know it, it is too late for them because either they are too old or their children have grown to stage that they cannot follow their parents everywhere as usual. Even many of the youths rebel from what their fathers or mothers are doing. Igbo Focus plans to amend all that by having a youth travel excursions to Igbo land. The travel excursions will compel these youths to cultivate interest in Igbo land and further bring in much needed investment in Igbo land. You may ask how that would bring investment. Many Igbo people abroad don’t want to invest in Igbo land due lack of interest from their family members. When asked to build a house, factory or open a branch office at home in Igbo land. The usual answer is “who am I building a house for; all of us are living comfortably here.” Or who will live in the house or manage the factory when I die. All are possible when a child or youth develops interest. Igbo land tourism Though money is the essence and impediment, otherwise Igbo Focus anticipated or hoped of arranging various tours to Igbo land and very similar tours from Igbo land to other counties, thus boosting tourism industry in Igbo land. Promoting Igbo Products As mentioned above that money is the essence and impediment, otherwise Igbo Focus anticipated of promoting Igbo products both at home and abroad. Those days are over, the time they used to criticise Igbo products as “Igbo made” just to degrade products made by Igbo people. Chinese and Japanese cars used to be in the same categories and today Datsun transformation into Nissan to become one of the best cars. Skoda was mimicked on as one of the slowest and worst cars ever but with BMW transformation, Skoda became one of the best cars. Along these lines, Igbo Focus continually is improving networks of announcements with every Igbo person, despite the fact perfecting best method of communications as well as adding new functionality and feature of technology - videos, website and social media.
