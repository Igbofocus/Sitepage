Fulani Terrorist Herdsmen Have Gone Killing Spree on People, Farms, Homes, Schools in Eha-Amufu, Enugu State
Written by Igbo Focus 8th April 2025 It is now troubling that these murderous bastards called “Fulani terrorist herdsmen” will come all the way from nowhere into the community under the pretence of being herders at the same time start killing people, despite that fact, public funds are wasted on police and army protecting them. Even, police and army prepared to put their lives, themselves on the line just to safeguard these Fulani terrorist bastards to do their killings just because someone somewhere is paying them. It looks like Fulani terrorist herdsmen will be armed and are encouraged to slaughter many people with impunity whilst their masters will enthusiastically shield them. Counter to these Fulani terrorist herdsmen, they are slaughtering people to make certain that many Nigerians are killed as they believe that Nigeria belongs to them and those they manage not to kill will be enslaved forever by them. In line with eye witnesses, Fulani terrorists first of all looted goods in the shops at Eboh Eha-Amufu Isi-Uzo Local government area of Enugu State and then set the shops and the remaining goods ablaze after killing over 100 innocent people. The Fulani terrorists were as well found at nearby farm suburbs, commonly known as “Ndiagu” where they attacked populaces killing almost 50 people compelling others to run for their life, abandoning their homes to seek refuge in the neighbouring areas. There were a lot of places affected by Fulani terrorists’ killings and such locations were the communities in Abor as well as in Umujoovu communities. Also, in Agu-Amede autonomous community; it was reported that almost ten farm communities were swamped, hijacked by Fulani terrorists. It was also the case in the Eha-Agu autonomous community where the farm hamlets were destroyed, which led the masses fleeing for their safety. The account is similarity with the same in the Mgbuji autonomous community, where farm villages were ejected from their communities, with residents having to relocate to Enugu metropolis or by staying with friends or family relations in other non-violent locations or secured neighbourhoods. As a result, the neighbourhoods are now totally dominated by Fulani terrorist herdsmen. It is despicable that none of the governors, especially those in the south east; south east governors or even Enugu State Governor, Peter Mbah is not talking about what is happening in his own state, all he is worried about is what Mazi Simon Ekpa who is in Finland is doing, at least Simon Ekpa is speaking up and he is troubled on what is going on in Peter Mbah’s own state. People must always talk about wrong doing, especially whenever injustice occurs, enough people ought to speak out against the evil done to other people. One Mohammad said “The killings by Fulani herdsmen that happened in the Bokkos communities in Plateau State, Eha-Amufu and Adani Enugu State is terrible. I have worked in Enugu and know how Fulani herdsmen have been killing people there. It’s very unfortunate that these things are happening and security agencies are doing nothing to stop it. I don’t understand how someone will compare the life of a cow to that of a human being.” - Faisal. It is a known fact that “when it comes to crimes being committed against the people of South and Christians the law is restricted, the North is silent, the law enforcement and military is gagged, and the victims are left to suffer alone but when the northerners become the victims of Nigerian syndrome and the societal decadence, the Nigerian conscience is jump started.” “Wickedness of Fulani terrorist killings should be condemned by everybody and it should not end there, government should move into action and demonstration that such actions will not be tolerated. The trouble with those in the North is that they push this narrative that everything in Nigeria is about them. They feel that no Hausa should be killed because they are innocent of everything as Fulanis are those causing the problems. It is difficult to differentiate between Hausa and Fulani people especially when Fulanis speak Hausa language. On account of this, when Fulanis are killed, they will claim that Hausas were those killed. One must ask, what are the Hausas going to hunt in Edo state that isn’t in the North. Have you seen any Edo hunters going with guns to hunt in the North. Why is it only Fulani Governors are those claiming that these people are hunters and they are Hausas. And why are they making so much anger over them as Nigerians are killed in their 100’s every day without any of these governors fusing about them. For example, someone said “Now check it out; if it were Igbo or Yoruba people that were found with ammunition claiming to be hunters going to hunt in the far north, what do you think would be their fate. And if you fact check the matter, you will believe me why the government is serious about this is because those terrorists were on a mission trip to take positions before more like them will arrive to carry out their wicked agenda. So killing them has shattered their planned attack.” Another person said “the problem of the Northern region and their leaders are lack of the knowledge of the rule of law and the ability to reason well like human beings. The question we should be asking the Northern hunters would be are you allowed to carry guns without licenses to another region for hunting. At that point in time, would that mean that anybody can wake up and carry guns to the bush and hunt without any clarification from the authority of that said state? Is Northern region more superior or above other states by carrying guns about from one state to the next hunting without approval from the police?” Inasmuch as the federal government is sustaining its position in supporting Fulani supremacy over other ethnic nationalities in Nigeria, what occurred in Edo state would be only a child's play. On the other hand, I was even surprise on the quick response of amnesty international concerning the killings of the 16 northerners in Edo state, while they found it difficult to do so in other killings in different parts of the country. Are they now in love with the north whilst loathing other parts? According to someone who said that from my own observation and point of view on happenings around Nigeria. There may possibly be the beginning of killing innocent Fulanis everywhere in Nigeria by other tribes. This is unavoidable until federal government fish out the bad eggs among the Fulanis that are killing people, sacking villages and destroying people's farm in the name of Fulanis. It will be a catastrophe not to address Fulani supremacy as this will cause more harm than Good. Those who are behind Fulani supremacy over other ethnic nationalities are the enemy of Nigeria and Africa at large, it must stop now before it's too late. Campaigning for Fulani supremacy, is an open call for anarchy in Nigeria because people will always rise to resist them by taking laws into their hands against Fulanis causing mayhem. At the present time, we are in democratic period of government for the people, when government failed to protect the citizens then citizens will protect themselves against the people that are coming to kill them. Those sponsoring Fulani supremacy should be aware that Fulani can only conquered the Hausas, which other ethnic nationalities are not willing to subject to for themselves to that of Hausa class, where Fulanis have subjected the Hausas as a second-class citizen in their own ancestral land. For those Northern political ideology scheming for Fulani supremacy will not work but then again can only work in the north, no other ethnic nationalities will tolerate it in the south or all hail will be let lose. The call now is to defend yourself. It is no longer a joke, forget about these Northern politicians sponsoring Fulani supremacy bribing western medias to get their stories across the world that Fulanis are being killed but it is the order way round. Defend yourself and your family as it has been made official. On the word of Bar. Aloy Ejimakor, “defend yourselves” is what Mazi Nnamdi Kanu said that was twisted to justify his extraordinary rendition and charging him for ‘incitement of violence’. It is also the same thing that led to the proscription of IPOB and the hounding of young Igbos as terrorists. But now DSS is saying it, the same thing Mazi Nnamdi Kanu said that put him in trouble. “Don’t expect army, police, others to protect all of you: Mobilise to defend yourselves against terrorists” DSS Chief told Nigerians. I believe the DSS DG made that statement upon hearing that the Fulani herdsmen’s excesses and is about reaching everybody in retaliation to what happened in Uromi. Perhaps if the threat was directed to the Igbos, the chief would have probably kept mute. Mazi Nnamdi Kanu is bigger than them all. It’s only a matter of time. For the so called DSS Chief remarkable fact and statement may be what the almighty God might use to exonerate Mazi Nnamdi Kanu’s freedom from his imprisonment for the reason that God has alternative measures as well as instruments to justify him. The DSS Chief made the statement due to the fact that it is well known fact now that Fulani terrorist herdsmen are killing people everywhere in the country and it is causing national and international humiliation to the government. Reacting to the above, someone said Nigeria has long lost its conscience due to hatred towards one particular nation known as Biafra. Please the defend yourself and your family and nation has been officially licensed by the federal government, so to your tent all states that is been tormented by terrorist attacks and herds men bandits, now no more excuses and protesting, just arm and defend yourself and your family. Other people’s lives matter. No one is superior than others and no tribes is superior than others. “When the government is quiet, the people feel the need to protect their lives and properties by themselves, which escalates issues and leads to the loss of other innocent lives. Injustice is injustice! It does not matter where it happens. North, East, South or West, we must always speak against it. “No person, group or tribe is superior to other tribe. All rights must be respected”. I would like you to read an article written by Femi Fani-Kayode about your so called ‘president’ now. Fami Fani- Kayode had known all along that the so called your president is a joker and purveyor of Fulani supremacy agenda. Yet, he encouraged his people to vote for him. And to the extent that he was championing his campaign during the election.
