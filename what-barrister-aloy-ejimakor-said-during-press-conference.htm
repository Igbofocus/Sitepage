What Barrister Aloy Ejimakor Said During Press Conference
After the press release by barrister Aloy Ejimakor and the rest of the legal team few days ago - I want to thank the barristers for saying it all very clear. Barrister Aloy Ejimakor said it all. All the history since our leader was abducted and renditioned. He mentioned some strong points as a reason why our leader's case turned to be a political case: 1. Our leader is being persecuted for at least 10 years because he is asking and demanding freedom for Biafra. His home land. He was already detained before for 18 months without a trial because of his broadcasts from his own legal radio station in England, where he lived as a local citizen, where anyone there can use his/her freedom of speech which is the most important right for every human being. Nothing like this happened before that a citizen of one country was abducted, detained and arrested in another country for saying his opinion on radio. 2. Short while after he was released and while he waited for court hearing - the Nigerian military invaded to his parents’ house as they committed their lethal attack!!! I'm sure that you understand that lethal attack is attack with intention to kill!!!! In their attempt to kill our leader during this LETHAL attack they killed 28 armless innocence IPOB members. Some Biafrans didn't hear well what the barrister said during the press release, or didn't understand what he said. He said lethal!!! Not little!!! I'm writing this to make it very clear. Everything that the barrister/s said was and is true! The zoo military had its lethal attack, which means intentionally killing our leader and everyone else in that house. Thank God that our leader run and saved his life. But it caused him to be wanted by the zoo government. Much worse happened later on. The zoo government proscribed IPOB as a terrorist organization. The UN rejected this prescription and decided that IPOB is not a terrorist organization and Mazi Nnamdi Kanu is not a terrorist leader. They ruled that his abduction and rendition is against the international law and recommend to release him. The courts in Umuahia and Abuja ruled that what the Nigerian government did to our leader was against the law and he should be free and get compensations from the government. All this is none of any interest of the Nigerian government and they are still holding our leader just because they can! That's why our leader's case turned to be a political case. All of the above is exactly the reason why it is a political case. Please - before you run or jump to judge wrongly - try to listen and understand what the barrister said, because I already saw the attacks on media. This is wrong. He said lethal!! Not little as some of you wrongly think and run to judge and attack. Barrister Aloy Ejimakor and the rest of the legal team were chosen by our leader and it is our leader's right. Not ours! We are not the ones that decide what's good or best for him. It’s his decision Only!!! There is no unity among Biafrans since our leader was abducted and renditioned. The energy is being channelled to wrong causes. There's hate, division and confusion. There are people that are trying to hijack the struggle using the disunity among Biafrans, and using IPOB's name and our leader's name to gain power and money while committing crimes. We must be focused and channel our energy to do the right thing and not being busy attacking. Thanks. Copied 26/01/2025
