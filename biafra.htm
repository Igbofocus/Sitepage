BIAFRA
Congratulations for the Declaration of United States of Biafra It is a welcome development for the declaration of the United States of Biafra (USB). The Biafra government in exile made it possible especially supervised by Dr. Ngozi Orabueze to declare USB from Finland. The UN Request to Release Mazi Nnamdi Kanu Nigeria is managed on lies and ineptitude. No one in government speaks the truth hence Nnamdi Kanu was kidnapped, tortured, renditioned and imprisoned. Since then, Nigerians have been calling on the government to do the honourable thing and release Nnamdi Kanu unconditionally however, this government is notorious. Circumstances of Nigeria Biafra war Background to the Nigeria Biafra War. The war which was fought from 1967 to 1970 that started over corruption, misrule, abuses of power, etc. Abuses of power were so rampant and corruptions were so endemic in Nigeria back then, encouraged by misguided political leaders that led to coup of 1966. Aburi Meeting (Aburi Accord) The Tape Recordings of Aburi Meeting that could have averted Nigeria Biafra war. Ghanaian President, Lieutenant-Colonel Joe Ankrah successfully hosted the meeting so that there will be harmony in Nigeria, but the war still ensued because Noth didn’t want the agreement. Even though, their leaders went to the Aburi meeting, signed the agreement but afterwards rejected the agreement.

The Biafra Story has been written by Frederick Forsyth who was a journalist in Biafra during the Nigeria Biafra War. For the copy of this book, search the internet as there are many publishers, sellers, and versions.
Surviving in Biafra - written by Alfred Uzokwe who witnessed the Nigeria Biafra war as a little boy during the war then. For a copy of this book, search the internet.
Half of a Yellow Sun was written by Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie. She wrote the book basing on stories told to her by her parents and others including her own research feedback on Nigeria Biafra War. For a copy of this book, search the internet for your preferred copy as there are many styles, publishers and sellers.
